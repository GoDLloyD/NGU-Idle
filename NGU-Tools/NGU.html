Explanation

Enter your equipment % stats in the following Format: Name,Type,eCap,mCap,ePow,mPow,NGU speed;next
Use the following Types: Head,Chest,Legs,Boots,Weapon,Acc
Enter the % bonuses of gear you don't want to include in the optimization progress, for example:
If you want to always wear Jakes Stapler, don't enter its values in the "Paste stuff here" box and instead enter its bonus in the Fixed Bonuses section. (60% eCap at lvl 100 -> enter "60" in "Fixed eCap Bonus%")
PS: You can choose "Name"
